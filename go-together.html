Traveller's Guide

Essential Tips for Travelling in India

🗓️

Best Time to Visit

October to March is ideal for most of India. Summers are great for hill stations like Manali and Shimla. Monsoon is best for Kerala and Goa.

💰

Budget Smart

India can be travelled on ₹1,500/day for budget trips. Book trains on IRCTC early. AC 3-Tier is the sweet spot for comfort vs cost.

🚆

Getting Around

Indian Railways connects almost every major city. For shorter trips, use state buses. Rickshaws and autos are great for local travel.

🍛

Food & Safety

Street food is incredible — eat where locals eat. Stick to fresh, hot food. Carry ORS packets and avoid tap water in new cities.

📱

Apps to Download

IRCTC for trains, RedBus for buses, Ola/Uber for cabs, MakeMyTrip for hotels, Google Maps for navigation.

🧳

What to Pack

Light cotton clothes for South/West India. Warm layers for North/Hill stations. Always carry a rain jacket, sunscreen, and a portable charger.

🛡️

Safety First

Keep photocopies of your ID. Share your itinerary with family. Use prepaid SIM cards for connectivity. Emergency number: 112.

🏛️

Respect Local Culture

Remove footwear at temples. Dress modestly at religious sites. Learn a few words of the local language — it goes a long way!
